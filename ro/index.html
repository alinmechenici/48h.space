#SpaceApps
Challenge

April 29-30 / Cluj-Napoca, Bucharest, Timisoara

Space Apps is an annual event that pulls together people regardless of their background or skill level.
Don't let the name fool you... it's not just about apps!
Tackle a challenge using robotics, data visualization, hardware, design and many other specialties!
Inspire each other while you learn and create using stories, code, design and, most of all, YOUR ideas.
Show us your problem-solving skills and share your talents with the world!

Make an impact!

Be part of this amazing experience of 48 hours of interdisciplinary work and all the coffee you can drink.
We know you have dreams! We do too. Our dream is to create the greatest Space Apps Challenge ever and make history!
Will you be a part of it?

Get on board!

You can register as a team if you'd like, or we'll get you synced up on the spot.
